LANGALANGA SEVENTH-DAY ADVENTIST CHURCH

Langalanga SDA Church is located in Nakuru City around Pangani area.Langalanga SDA has a more than 500 members approximately and this makes it a big and wonderful church.

Welcome all to Langalanga SDA Church,Feel at the feet of Jesus

Pastor's and Elder's Office

Pastor John Mwangi is the spiritual father to Langalanga SDA Church members, He is welcoming,accomodative and with the help of Elders meets the spiritual growth of church memebers.

Music Department

The purpose of the Music Department is to provide and maintain spiritually uplifting vocal and instrumental music that will enhance the worship experience.

Youth's Department

The purpose of the Youth Ministries Department is to guide the youth into having a saving relationship with Jesus Christ, and to develop constantly into disciples, which will be reflected in a life of service in the church and in the community.
